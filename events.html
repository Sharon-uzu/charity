About Tech-230 Project

The Tech-230 Project, an initiative by the Chukwudi Dimkpa Foundation, is a groundbreaking effort to empower 230 youths in Rivers State with in-demand tech skills. With a transparent selection process and training by a reputable Tech Hub, participants choose from programs like Product Design, CyberSecurity, Software engineering, Graphic Design, Data Analytics, etc.

This legacy project extends beyond training, offering co-working spaces, internships, and access to esteemed tech platforms, fostering entrepreneurship and creating a lasting impact.

ABOUT TECH-230

How It Works

Selection

230 youths from Rivers State will be selected to benefits from the scholarship. An email will be sent to selected candidates.

Orientation

Selected candidates will attend a compulsory one-day orientation program. It is a program to usher us into the training proper.

Training Begins

The selected candidates will be trained intentionally for 3 months on the various tech skills they selected during their registration.

After training support

Beneficiaries that successfully commits till the end of the training will get a well-coordinated after traoining support.
